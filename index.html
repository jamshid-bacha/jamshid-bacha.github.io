About Me

I am Jamshid Bacha. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. under the supervision of the renowned Prof. Falko Dressler in the field of Telecommunications and Networking. I am working as a Research Scientist at the Technical University of Berlin, Germany, focusing on Reinforcement Learning Research for Wireless Communication, Radio Resource Management and Wi-Fi 7. I accomplished my MS degree from Korea Aerospace University, advised by Prof. Sungchang Lee. Before joining TU Berlin, I was a fwllow researcher at CCR Lab, working on Machine Learning and Computer Vision research for COVID-19 patient distance detection and classification, object detection, offensive text detection, and social media plugins.
